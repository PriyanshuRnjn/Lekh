## 🔒 Private by Design

> **Your billing data is not the product.**

LEKH is built with a simple philosophy: your business data belongs to you.

| What LEKH does | What LEKH never does |
|---|---|
| ✅ Stores all data locally on your device | ❌ Sells your billing data |
| ✅ Works completely offline for daily billing | ❌ Sells your customer data |
| ✅ Keeps business records private | ❌ Uses tracking-first design |
| ✅ Designed for Indian business workflows | ❌ Forces live sync for daily work |
